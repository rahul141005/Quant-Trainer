📖 App Guide

Your quick guide to mastering Quant Reflex Trainer

Getting Started

🚀 Overview

Quant Reflex Trainer is designed to build mental math speed and quantitative reflexes for competitive exams like CAT, CET, and banking tests.

Train daily to build lasting habits and measurable improvement.

App Tabs

🏠 Home

  • Daily Warmup — Quick 5-question session to start your day
  • Progress Snapshot — Today's attempts, accuracy, and streaks at a glance
  • Daily Goal — Track your progress toward your daily question target
  • Quick Study — Customizable shortcuts to your favorite learning sections

🎯 Practice

  • Quick Drill — 5 questions, no timer — perfect for quick warmups
  • Reflex Drill — 10 questions with 15s per question — trains speed
  • Timed Test — 10 questions in 3 minutes — simulates exam pressure
  • Focus Training — Practice a specific category to target weak areas
  • Review Mistakes — Retry previously wrong answers until mastered

📖 Learn

  • Fraction → Percentage — Memorize common conversions
  • Multiplication Tables — Quick reference tables 1–30
  • Quant Formulas — Profit & Loss, Ratios, Averages, TSD
  • Shortcut Tricks — Mental math techniques for faster solving
  • Custom Formulas — Add, edit, and organize your own notes

💡 Triple-tap any multiplication table to open a larger modal view.

📊 Stats

  • Category Accuracy — Performance breakdown per topic
  • Weakest Category — Identifies areas needing the most work
  • Response Times — Tracks your average answering speed
  • Streaks & Trends — Monitor daily consistency and improvement

⚙️ Settings

Customize your experience with toggles for dark mode, themes, sound, vibration, notifications, difficulty level, and daily goal targets.
